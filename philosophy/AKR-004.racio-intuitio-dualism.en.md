# Racio and Intuitio: Dual Epistemology in OCAM

## 1. Introduction

OCAM distinguishes two complementary modes of knowing:

- **Racio** — rational analysis  
- **Intuitio** — ontological insight  

---

## 2. Racio

Logical, sequential, empirical.  
Structures the manifested.

---

## 3. Intuitio

Direct contact with essence.  
Reveals the inner nature of phenomena.

---

## 4. Dual Imperative

Racio structures → Intuitio discloses.  
Together → full cognition.

---

## 5. In Akashi Records

Dual validation:

- Racio-validation: structure, hash, metadata  
- Intuitio-validation: ontology, essence, phenomenology  

Forms a **dual-layer archive**.
